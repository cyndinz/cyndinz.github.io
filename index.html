Gaafree

I truly believe there should be a portal where we can come together to explore and connect over all these incredible ideas.

Unitarity

The principle of unitarity says that information about a system’s initial state is preserved in its final state. Even if it transforms wildl via entropy or black holes, it's still encoded in the system somehow.
Stephen Hawking initially proposed that Hawking radiation doesn't carry information thus information could be lost in black holes.
Later theory holographic principle invented by Gerard 't Hooft, developed and formalized by Leonard Susskind, and mathematically backed by Juan Maldacena suggests that information isn't lost but somehow smeared on the event horizon or encoded in the Hawking radiation.
Hence, my question: information can be radiated out? Recoverable? Reversible?
