Gaafree

Gaafree has no concrete definition - at least, not yet. It’s something that has lingered in my mind for quite some time, a concept shaped by the enchantment of the magical universe and the brilliance of science. I truly believe there should be a portal where we can come together to explore and connect over all these incredible ideas.

Waltz

The stars are dancing, bright and free, a waltz of fire in endless sea.
They spin and shimmer, burn and gleam, a silent song, a golden dream.
The planets turn in measured flight, bound by forces, locked in light.
Yet in their glow, a truth we find - we're but a spark in space and time.

Infinity

No edge, no end, no final line, a silent stretch beyond the time.
The stars dissolve, yet still they glow, in endless paths we’ll never know.
A breath, a pulse, a ceaseless stream, forevermore, a boundless dream.
For all we are and all we'll be, is but a wave upon the infinity.
