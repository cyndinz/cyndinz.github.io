🌿 Calathea Zebrina

Date added: 11/12/2024

  • 🦓 Named for its bold zebra-like stripes — a natural camouflage for rainforest life.
  • ⏰ Belongs to the prayer plant family — its leaves raise and lower with the light!
  • 💧 Sensitive to chemicals in tap water — prefers rain or distilled water.

🌱 Calathea Network

Date added: 30/03/2025

  • 🕸️ Leaves have a mesmerizing digital-like net pattern — hence the name "Network."
  • 🔍 Nature’s camouflage expert: its pattern hides it from predators in the wild.
  • 🕺 Moves gently with the light — it does a little leaf dance throughout the day!

🌿 Alocasia Stingray

Date added: 13/04/2025

  • 🐠 Its unique leaf looks just like a stingray — with fins and a curling tail!
  • 🧬 Related to giant-leaf species that grow umbrella-sized in the wild!
  • 🐘 Nicknamed “Elephant Ear” — but this one's got a stingray twist!
