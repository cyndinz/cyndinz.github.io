Calathea Zebrina

Date added: 11/12/2024

This striking plant has vibrant green leaves with dark zebra-like stripes. It's known for its dramatic leaf movement and is perfect for bright indoor spaces with indirect light.

Calathea Network

Date added: 30/03/2025

Featuring a mesmerizing network pattern on its leaves, this Calathea is a true showstopper. Like all prayer plants, its leaves move up and down with the light throughout the day.

Alocasia Stingray

Date added: 13/04/2025

This unique Alocasia features leaves that resemble the wings and tail of a stingray. It brings an exotic vibe and loves humidity and bright, indirect light.
